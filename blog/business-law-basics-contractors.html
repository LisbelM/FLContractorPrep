Business Law Basics Every Contractor Needs to Know

Jan 5, 2024 10 min read

Business law shows up on the Florida GC exam because it directly affects how you operate and get paid. Focus on these core areas first.

Contracts and scope

Know the elements of a valid contract, change order basics, and how scope impacts pricing and disputes.

Liens and notices

Lien rules are common exam questions. Learn the basic notice timelines, who can file, and how to preserve lien rights.

Licensing and compliance

Review the licensing requirements, renewal deadlines, and the penalties for unlicensed activity.

Insurance and risk

Expect questions on general liability, workers compensation, and when certificates of insurance are required.

Project documentation

Daily logs, pay applications, and closeout documentation protect your business and often show up on the exam.
